Welcome to PixelQuest Trade

PixelQuest Trade, is a nonprofit organization established under Section 501(c)(7) of the Internal Revenue Code. We are dedicated to fostering community engagement, promoting digital inclusion, and empowering individuals through innovative trade initiatives.

Our Mission

To serve as a catalyst for community enlightenment and growth by leveraging digital platforms to promote social well-being and community engagement.

Our Purpose

  • Online Community Building: Creating welcoming digital spaces for members to connect and share ideas.
  • Digital Inclusion: Bridging the digital divide by providing resources to underserved communities.
  • Future Growth: Raising funds to host local events, establish community hubs, and support charitable initiatives.

Key Activities

Our activities are designed to engage members, foster creativity, and promote positive social change:

  • Hosting virtual events and fundraisers.
  • Showcasing talents through community showcases.
  • Providing access to physical community hubs and resources.
  • Supporting charitable causes and initiatives.

Management and Governance

PixelQuest Trade is managed by its members, Board, or elected officials in accordance with its operating agreement. Our leadership is committed to transparency and upholding our values in all activities.
