Meet Our Trailblazers

These individuals have gone above and beyond to support PixelQuest Trade. Their dedication, generosity, and belief in our mission have been instrumental in our success.

Stacy Simental

Stacy Simental is a cornerstone of PixelQuest Trade’s journey. As the first to donate and test our internal systems, Stacy’s unwavering belief in our project has been a source of inspiration. Her contributions and support allowed us to focus on bringing our vision to life. We are deeply grateful for her dedication and generosity.

Patti Nolan

Patti Nolan, a celebrated jazz vocalist, brings her creative spirit to PixelQuest Trade. Her journey from Montana to international stages and back home inspires us all. Patti has graciously agreed to be featured on our site, showcasing the power of artistic expression and resilience. Her support fuels our mission to create inclusive and creative communities.
