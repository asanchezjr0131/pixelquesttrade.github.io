Welcome to PixelQuest Trade Membership

Join our growing community of innovators and adventurers! Your membership supports our mission to bridge the digital divide, foster creativity, and build inclusive spaces for community growth.

Proceeds go towards:

  • Expanding access to underserved communities.
  • Hosting events and activities that bring people together.
  • Developing resources like internet hubs, job assistance programs, and educational workshops.

Membership Tiers

Free Supporter

Price: $0/three months

Benefits:

  • Access to public content and newsletters.
  • Invitations to free community events.
  • Show your support for our mission at no cost.

Voyager

Price: $12/three months (only $4 per month)

Benefits:

  • All Free Supporter benefits.
  • Access to members-only forums and discussions.
  • Priority invitations to select events.
  • Eligible for exclusive future promotions.

Explorer

Price: $33/three months (only $11 per month)

Benefits:

  • All Voyager benefits.
  • Free access to select virtual events.
  • Exclusive digital content, such as tutorials and behind-the-scenes updates.
  • Eligible for higher-tier future promotions.

Trailblazer (Top Tier)

Price: $60/three months (only $20 per month)

Benefits:

  • All Explorer benefits.
  • VIP access to all events (virtual and physical).
  • Personalized thank-you gifts.
  • Special recognition on our platform.
  • Eligible for premium future promotions.

Why Choose PixelQuest Trade Membership?

Your membership helps us:

  • Provide digital resources and support to underserved communities.
  • Host inclusive and creative events that foster connection and collaboration.
  • Build and sustain spaces that inspire growth and empowerment.
